Audacity Instructions

Make Recording
  1. Click "Record" button before the sermon starts
  2. Click "Stop" button after the closing prayer (or trim after the prayer finishes)
Trim
  1. Trim deadspace before sermon starts and after final prayer
  2. Select and drag cursor over section and then hit "Delete" on keyboard
Normalize
  1. Select All audio (either Ctrl+A or mouse or Edit menu)
  2. Select "Normalize" from menu
  4. Use preset settings we already have configured
Export
  1. Export MP3 from menu
  3. Name file as YYYYMMDD.mp3 with today's date
  4. Click Save and fill in metadata when prompted
